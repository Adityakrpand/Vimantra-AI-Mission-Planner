# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive engineering environment. Contributors, maintainers, users, and reviewers are expected to collaborate with care and assume good intent while maintaining high technical standards.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Respect different levels of experience and domain knowledge.
- Give constructive, specific feedback.
- Prioritize safety, clarity, and evidence in technical decisions.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them transparently.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive comments unrelated to the work.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass safety, testing, or review requirements.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent removal from project participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the support or security channels documented in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) and [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports should include context, links, and any relevant screenshots or logs.
